**Project Description**: An interactive dashboard analyzing global demand and supply for digital and AI jobs across countries, industries, and skill types. By combining World Bank labor data with Stanford's HAI Index, JobsLens AI helps policymakers identify skills gaps, benchmark workforce readiness, and make data-driven decisions on education and workforce development in the rapidly evolving digital economy.
